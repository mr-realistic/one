TxC

TxC (short for Tarryn and Clair) is a dynamic female DJ duo from South Africa making waves in the Amapiano scene. Known for their high-energy performances and bold presence, TXC brings both style and skill to the decks, representing a fresh and powerful voice in a male-dominated space. They’re best known for their viral DJ sets, choreography, and fashion-forward image, often blending performance and party into one unforgettable experience. Their mixes are rooted in Bacardi-infused Amapiano, featuring heavy log drums, catchy rhythms, and street bangers that get the crowd moving. TXC have performed on big stages across South Africa and internationally, quickly becoming icons for young women in music and nightlife culture. While they’ve gained fame through their live sets, they’re also stepping deeper into music production and collaborations with well-known artists in the scene. In short, TXC isn’t just about the music — they’re about movement, energy, and representation, showing the world that women can lead and dominate in the Amapiano wave too.
