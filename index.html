SCORPION KINGS

The Scorpion Kings are a South African music duo made up of Dj Maphorisa and Kabza De Small, known for popularizing the Amapiano genre. Since teaming up in 2019, they've released hit songs like “Abalele,” “eMcimbini,” “Sponono,” and “Lorch.” Their unique blend of deep house, kwaito, and jazz influences has made them local legends and global pioneers. They've collaborated with major artists and played a key role in taking Amapiano to the world stage
